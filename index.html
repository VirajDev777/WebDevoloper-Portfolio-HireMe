About Clinic

Satya Physiotherapy Clinic in Dombivli is dedicated to restoring movement, reducing pain, and helping patients regain a healthy and active lifestyle. Using modern techniques and personalized care plans, the clinic focuses on long-term recovery rather than temporary relief.

About Dr. Sayali Khare

Dr. Sayali Khare is a highly skilled and experienced physiotherapist based in Dombivli, Maharashtra. She specializes in treating joint pain, muscle injuries, post-surgery rehabilitation, and sports injuries. With a patient-first approach, Dr. Khare carefully analyzes each case and designs customized treatment plans that ensure faster recovery and long-term results. Her calm nature and professional expertise make patients feel comfortable and confident during their treatment journey. She is known for: ✔ Accurate diagnosis ✔ Modern physiotherapy techniques ✔ Friendly and supportive care ✔ Effective home exercise guidance Her mission is simple — help every patient move pain-free and live better.
